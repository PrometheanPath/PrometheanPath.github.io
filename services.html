What we do

Seven ways we help you succeed.

01

University Advisor Connection

Connect with advisors from your exact target university who potentially studied your degree. Get real insights into what the course, campus, and culture are actually like, from people who have already made the decisions you are trying to make.

02

Career Advisor Connection

Connect with advisors who recently studied your path and now hold the roles you are aiming for. Understand what the hiring process actually involves, what firms are genuinely looking for, and the decisions that shaped their route from student to professional.

03

Career Application Guidance

Get clarity on what firms and early career recruiters are actually looking for, from people who have already been through it. We demystify Assessment Centres, Superdays, video and in-person interviews, written exercises, online assessments and the selection criteria before you even walk in.

04

Personal Statement Review

Get direct, honest feedback on your personal statement from people who know what admissions teams actually want to see. No generic advice, instead specific input from those who went through the same process and know what works.

05

CV & Cover Letter Review

Get a professional polish on your application materials. Get feedback from people who understand what graduate recruiters look for and how to make your experiences stand out on paper.

06

Academic Performance

Get guidance on how to consistently perform at the highest level at university by learning the study habits, exam techniques, and approaches used by first-class students, plus receive detailed feedback on your Formatives, Summatives, and Dissertations.

07

General Guidance

Unsure about what you need? Connect with us and we will help you figure out the best way we can support you, whether that is with one of the above or something more specific to your situation.
